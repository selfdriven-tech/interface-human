  • Reporting
  • On-Chain
  • Periodic reporting in the form of JSON data attached to a shared project.
  • Members get access to the shared data by being added to the project.
  • They can authenticate using username/password or by signing data (email address) using a Cardano wallet.
  • Data includes posts from "Reporting" conversation.
  • On-chain hashes of the reporting data minted using the organisations reporting policy ID.
  • Based on the files (data) attached to the shared projects.
  • Acting as verifiable creditionals (VC) linked to the organisation DID.

Community

Improve

Propose a project that will improve your community.

This is not about ideas as "thought bubbles", as those are easily obtained.
The program is for people or a group of people in your learning community that have a well-formed idea for improvement and want to make it happen, with guidance and professional assistance when required.
You use the getting started form to submit your proposal for an improvement with basic information, including why it will create an improvement and with as much of the what/how/who details that you have formed. Once you submit the improvement proposal an online document will be created and key people notified that can help you with the process.
